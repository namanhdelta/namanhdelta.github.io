Careers

Join our team and help shape the future of science & technology

We are always looking for driven, curious, and detail-oriented people to join our growing team. Whether you are an experienced researcher or a student ready to take on your first professional challenge, we have a place for you at NAMANH DELTA.

We operate across Brussels (Belgium), Ho Chi Minh City, Ha Noi, and Can Tho — offering a truly international, cross-disciplinary environment where science meets real-world impact.

Researcher

Project-Based Master / PhD

We are seeking project-based researchers at Master's or PhD level to collaborate on robotics projects. Contract duration and scope vary by project.

What You Will Do

  • Conduct applied research within an assigned project scope, delivering high-quality outputs on time
  • Review and synthesise scientific literature relevant to the project domain
  • Design experiments, collect and analyse data, and draw evidence-based conclusions
  • Prepare technical reports, presentations, and — where applicable — publications
  • Collaborate closely with internal team members, academic partners, and clients
  • Contribute to proposal writing and documentation for follow-on projects

Requirements

  • Master's degree (completed or in progress) or PhD in a relevant scientific or engineering discipline
  • Demonstrated ability to work independently and deliver within project timelines
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Proficiency in relevant research tools, software, or laboratory methods depending on domain
  • Comfortable working across cultures and time zones in an international team
  • Exceptional attention to detail is an absolute requirement — accuracy and rigour in all outputs is non-negotiable

Locations

Ho Chi Minh City  ·  Can Tho

How to Apply

Send your CV and a short cover letter (maximum one page) to contact@namanhdelta.com with Researcher in the subject line. Please indicate your preferred location, availability, and the domain you wish to work in.

We review applications on a rolling basis and will contact shortlisted candidates directly.

Internships

5 Open Positions Internship

We offer hands-on internship opportunities for students and recent graduates who are eager to apply their academic knowledge to real projects.

General Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a Bachelor's, Master's, or equivalent programme in a relevant discipline — including but not limited to computer science, automation, mechatronics, or biomedical engineering.
  • Strong curiosity and a proactive, self-starter attitude
  • Good written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Ability to manage your own workload and meet deadlines
  • Team player who also works well independently
  • Basic proficiency in standard office tools (documents, spreadsheets, presentations)
  • High attention to detail is a must — we expect precision and care in everything you produce, from data entries to written summaries

Locations

Brussels, Belgium  ·  Ho Chi Minh City  ·  Ha Noi  ·  Can Tho
Exact location is agreed per position and candidate profile.

How to Apply

Send your CV and a short cover letter (maximum one page) to contact@namanhdelta.com with Internship – [Discipline] in the subject line (e.g. Internship – Mechatronics). Please indicate your preferred location and availability.

We review applications on a rolling basis and will contact shortlisted candidates directly.
